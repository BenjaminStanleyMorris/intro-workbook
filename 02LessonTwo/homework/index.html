About Me

I was born in Alabama and I lived there for most of my life. It's an ok place if you like nature, but there ain't much going on. I guess I always figured that leaving was the best option.
The older I get I dunno if there even was a best option. I went away for a while. A long way away for a long while. I spent some time in the Himalayas and in Myanmar.
There was a small stint in a gypsy collective in Denmark. I came back to America and now I am in Texas, oil and computers. I figure oil will go away, but computers never will.
